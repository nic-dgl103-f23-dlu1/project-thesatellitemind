Vendors

Vendor applications go live February 1, 2024

Over the past several years Vancouver Island MusicFest has made a conscious effort to demonstrate our commitment to presenting a cultural event where the principles of fair trade, low environmental footprint and quality product prevail. Our vendor jury looks carefully at all aspects of your vendor application to understand clearly where you source your product and the impact your product will have on the natural environment â€“ both at the fairgrounds and at the source. All of our food vendors are required to have biodegradable, compostable or recyclable food service materials (cutlery, plates, bowls, mugs, packages) for all of their operations. This is an important part of our efforts to move towards a zero-waste event and it is the standard already in place for our internal kitchens.

Volunteers

Volunteer Handbook will be available June 15, 2024

We welcome over 1100 volunteers to our team each year and we'd love to have you join our crew!

Volunteers are the lifeblood of our organization and we try to place as many new volunteers as possible. If we can't place you this year we will put your name on our waitlist and contact you as soon as a crew opening becomes available.

If you are 16-19 years of age you will need to have a youth waiver signed by your parents or guardian.
